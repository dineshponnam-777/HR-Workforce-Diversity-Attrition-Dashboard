# 📊 HR Workforce Diversity & Attrition Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This Tableau dashboard analyzes workforce demographics, employee attrition trends, tenure distribution, and employee satisfaction across departments.

The objective is to help HR professionals and business leaders identify workforce patterns, understand attrition drivers, and support data-driven talent management decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

✔ Analyze gender diversity across departments

✔ Identify departments with high employee attrition

✔ Understand attrition patterns by age group

✔ Evaluate employee satisfaction levels

✔ Analyze workforce tenure distribution

✔ Support HR decision-making through visual analytics

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

* Tableau Public
* Microsoft Excel / CSV Dataset
* Data Visualization
* HR Analytics

---

## 📈 Dashboard Components

### 1️⃣ Department-wise Gender Diversity

* IT department has the largest workforce.
* Female representation remains significant across major departments.
* Gender distribution insights support diversity monitoring initiatives.

### 2️⃣ Age Group Distribution

* Employees aged 35–44 represent the largest workforce segment.
* Employees aged 25–34 form the second-largest group.
* Workforce is primarily concentrated within mid-career age brackets.

### 3️⃣ Satisfaction Analysis

* Average satisfaction scores remain relatively stable across departments.
* No department shows critically low satisfaction levels.
* Operations and R&D demonstrate slightly higher satisfaction scores.

### 4️⃣ Attrition by Department

* IT department records the highest employee attrition.
* Sales and Operations also show elevated attrition levels.
* R&D exhibits the lowest attrition among major departments.

### 5️⃣ Attrition by Age Group

* Highest attrition is observed among employees aged 35–44.
* Employees aged 25–34 follow closely.
* Attrition decreases significantly for employees above 45 years.

### 6️⃣ Employee Count by Tenure Group

* Employees with 3–5 years tenure form the largest workforce segment.
* 6–10 years tenure group also represents a substantial portion.
* Workforce size declines after 10 years of tenure.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

📌 IT Department has the highest attrition count.

📌 Workforce is concentrated in the 25–44 age range.

📌 Most employees have between 3–10 years of experience.

📌 Satisfaction scores remain consistent across departments.

📌 Gender diversity is well represented across business functions.
